Product Design & Development

I built a youth sports platform from scratch. With zero coding experience.

Lynx started as a solution for my own volleyball club, Black Hornets. I was tired of managing a competitive youth program with group texts, spreadsheets, and five different apps. So I designed and built a dual-platform product: React + Vite for the web admin, React Native + Expo for the mobile app, sharing a Supabase backend with 60+ database tables. From zero experience to a fully functional product.

The Differentiator

Youth sports has an engagement problem. Lynx fixes it.

Every youth sports management app handles the basics: rosters, schedules, payments. But none of them solve the real problem: keeping kids engaged in their own development and making parents feel connected to the experience. That's the gap Lynx was built to fill.

The engagement layer is what makes Lynx different. It sits on top of the management tools and transforms how players, coaches, parents, and admins interact with the club. It's designed to feel like a sports video game, not a spreadsheet.

For Players (Ages 10-18)

Development that feels like a game.

Most kids get a stat sheet at the end of the season and forget about it. Lynx makes development something players actually want to engage with.

XP Power Bars

Skill evaluations displayed as color-tiered XP bars. Sky blue to gold for excellent, gray for below average. Never plain numbers or boring charts.

Challenge System

25+ pre-built challenge templates that coaches deploy to keep players developing between practices. Skill drills, conditioning goals, team competitions.

Achievement Badges

Inspired by gaming culture. Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary tiers. Players unlock badges through evaluations, challenges, and milestones. It's a reward system that makes development visible.

Player Profile

Feels like a sports video game character screen, not a database entry. Streak tracking, leaderboards, team standings, and a reason to come back every day.

For Coaches

Replace the clipboard. Run game day from your phone.

Player Evaluations

12 volleyball skills with swipe-through mobile forms. Evaluations feed directly into player XP bars and progression.

Game Day Command Center

Lineup building, real-time attendance, score tracking, and stat entry all in one flow. Designed for the sideline, not the office.

Challenge Deployment

Deploy challenges to keep players developing between practices. Track completion, review results, reward effort.

Communication Hub

Team messaging with parent communication built in. Schedule practices and games with RSVP tracking. No more group texts.

For Parents

No more "what happened at practice?"

Parents are the lifeblood of youth sports, but most clubs treat them like ATMs who sit in bleachers with no visibility. Lynx changes that.

Full Visibility

See your child's evaluations, achievements, and progress in real time. Not just payment reminders.

Schedule & RSVP

Practice and game schedules with one-tap RSVP. Never miss a game or wonder "when do we need to be there?"

Payments & Registration

Payment tracking, multi-child registration, digital waivers. All managed through Stripe.

Coach Communication

Direct channel to coaches. Updates, announcements, and conversations in one place. No more scattered group texts.

For Admins

One platform that handles everything.

Club directors shouldn't need spreadsheets, paper forms, and three different systems to run a season. Lynx puts it all in one place.

Season Management

Create seasons, build teams, manage rosters. Multi-team, multi-season support.

Registration & Waivers

Custom registration forms with digital waivers. Multi-child registration for families with multiple athletes.

Custom Dashboard

Drag-and-drop widget customization. Role-based views. See exactly what matters to you.

Operations

Jersey management, payment tracking, volunteer coordination. The admin work that used to take days now takes minutes.

Tech Stack

What powers Lynx.

Built entirely through AI-assisted development with Claude as the development partner. Zero coding background to a fully functional dual-platform product.

React + Vite

Web admin dashboard. Fast, modern, and responsive. Runs on any browser.

React Native + Expo

Mobile app for iOS and Android. Shared codebase, native performance.

Supabase

Backend with 60+ database tables. Real-time data, role-based access, authentication.

Stripe

Payment processing for registration fees, season dues, and event payments.

The Takeaway

The best products are built by people who live the problem.

I'm not a software engineer. I'm a builder who happened to need software. Lynx exists because I spent years running a club, got tired of the tools available, and decided to build something better. That same energy is how I approach every project, whether it's a training program, a recruiting function, or a full product. If it's broken, I'll fix it. If it's missing, I'll build it.
