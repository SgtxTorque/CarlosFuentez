The Other Stuff

"The stuff that doesn't fit on a resume."

5+ Years and Counting

Volleyball is where it clicks.

I'm a Director and Head Coach at Black Hornets Volleyball Club in Dallas. I didn't start with the title. I earned it over 5+ years of competitive youth volleyball coaching by developing young players and young adults, managing expectations from athletes and parents alike, and getting instruction to click.

Coaching connects directly to my professional work. The same skills that make me effective in a training room make me effective on a volleyball court. Breaking down complex skills into teachable moments. Reading a room and knowing when to push and when to pull back. Designing practices that are engaging enough to hold attention but structured enough to produce results.

I'm a natural teacher and facilitator. Getting someone to learn something is about meeting them where they are and making the information land. That's true whether I'm coaching a 12-year-old on serve receive or training a store manager on a new hiring process.

The Setup

Where it all happens.

The plants. The ultrawide. The ambient lighting. This isn't just a desk. It's the cockpit. This is where Lynx was designed, where training programs were written at midnight, where recruiting campaigns were edited and brand guides were built.

The vibe matters. A well-designed workspace isn't just aesthetic. It's functional. Everything is intentional, from the monitor placement to the lighting. It's the same philosophy I bring to everything I build: make it work beautifully.

The People

Family first. Always.

Everything I build, everything I coach, everything I chase comes back to the people I care about. The drive doesn't come from nowhere. It comes from wanting better for the people around me.

"This is who I am when nobody's watching."

The same energy I bring to a gym full of athletes is the same energy I bring to a boardroom, a training session, or a product build. It's not a switch. It's just me. I want to see the team win.
