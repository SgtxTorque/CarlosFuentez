4 RESTOCK SCHEDULE — NON-NEGOTIABLES
Time What to Restock Why It Matters Done
Opening Full cooler restock, snack bars faced forward, retail display straightened, POS drawer verified Early birds buy the most drinks. Empty cooler = lost revenue.
Mid-Day Restock drinks sold during AM, wipe counters, refill napkins/straws, face all products Lunch crowd hits hard. Be ready before the wave, not during.
Closing Full cooler restock for next day, clean blender area, wipe all surfaces, count inventory, close POS The next shift inherits your ProShop. Leave it EPIC.
